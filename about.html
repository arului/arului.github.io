About me

I am actively trying to get better with React/Vue/Angular and this whole 'front end' craze. I'm not a super creative person when it comes to UI looks, but I enjoy creating something usable and nice looking from front to back.

I am most comfortable with full stack applications, primarily used with backend web frameworks such as Java and J2EE based frameworks. I worked heavily in the backend, and infrastructure portion of many of my jobs and projects. That means I also mess around with Jenkins, CI/CD, google cloud and all that fun jazz.

Outside of the office I like to keep active, taking part in a multitude of activities and have a strong passion for travelling.
